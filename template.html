Social media login

Use Facebook Connect or another single sign on technology solution to allow your customers to use their social media logins to sign into the mobile app. And always give them a way to retrieve their user name/password.

Prioritize speed

It’s very important to make sure the app isn’t slow. People used to despise Facebook because of how slow the mobile app is. It is crucial that your app doesn’t make people wait around while it loads.

Include analytics

As a mobile app developer, one key component is to incorporate analytics into your mobile app.Most users do not enjoy giving up their location, which is understandable. Tracking a users location is different to tracking and analyzing their expe rience.

FAQ.Most frequently asked questions.

How do I backup, restore or transfer my data?
You can backup your Wikiapp data using Google Drive and local backup. It will run automatically every day at 2am and save your database in a file on the phone itself.
Why cant I connect to Wikiapp?
Usually this is caused by a problem with your internet connection or the settings on your phone.
How do I reinstall Wikiapp?
You can reinstall by Go to Settings > Applications > Wikiapp > Install .
When I travel abroad, will I be charged for dara roaming?
Wikiapp uses the same internet data plan as web browsing and email on your phone.
How do I delete my acccount ?
Follow the steps to delete your account. Go to Settings > Account > Delete my account.
